Meet Our Partners

Who: Ring Line Corporation
Location: Taiwan
Product: Zimbra Collaboration

A value added reseller of Zimbra Collaboration in the Asia Pacific region, Ring Line Corporation offers systems integration services, network construction and administration, information security and application development.

“By upgrading the legacy messaging system to Zimbra Collaboration, not only did Ring Line Corporation completely eradicate existing challenges, the company also incorporated various collaboration task tools and facilitated internal communication and operation processes,” said XUE Yindong, CTO and COO, Ring Line Corporation.

Key benefits of the Zimbra Collaboration upgrade:

  • Improved client relationships
  • Established online support
  • Collaborative R&D and innovation

Ring Line Corporation is headquartered in Taipei , with five additional locations throughout Taiwan.
